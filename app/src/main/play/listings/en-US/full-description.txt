Locky is a tiny, native app locker that guards your private apps behind your fingerprint — and nothing else. No PIN, no pattern, no cloud account, no ads, no tracking. Just your fingerprint.

WHAT LOCKY DOES
• Lock any installed app. When you open it, a full-screen gate asks for your fingerprint before it opens.
• Cancel the prompt and you go back to your home screen — never into the locked app.
• Re-locks automatically. The moment a locked app leaves the screen (Home, app switch, recents, or screen off) it locks again, so it always asks next time.
• Entry gate. Opening Locky itself needs your fingerprint too.
• Home-screen shortcuts. Long-press Locky's icon for quick "Lock an app" / "Unlock an app" actions.

BUILT TO BE SMALL AND HONEST
• Pure Kotlin + Android Views + Material 3. No heavy frameworks. Small download.
• 100% on-device. Your list of locked apps is stored only on your phone.
• No internet permission used for your data. No analytics. No accounts.

PERMISSIONS LOCKY NEEDS (and why)
• Display over other apps — to draw the unlock screen on top of a locked app.
• Accessibility — to notice when a locked app opens so it can show the unlock screen. Locky does NOT read screen content (window-content retrieval is turned off).
• Fingerprint (biometric) — to unlock.

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 11 or newer.
• A fingerprint enrolled on your device (Locky is fingerprint-only by design — there is no PIN fallback).

Locky keeps it simple: one job, done natively, with no data leaving your phone.
